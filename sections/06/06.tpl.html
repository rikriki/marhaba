simple connections

Dubai is an international crossroads for passengers travelling all over the world. Our citystop and transfer services make stopping over in the city, or transferring ﬂights much easier.

citystop

For stopovers in Dubai, our citystop service assists with obtaining a 96-hour visa, hotel reservations and ground transfers. We can also provide additional assistance at the airport and offer a special citystop package for those en route to the Holy Land.

Transfer

When changing planes, we can meet customers and help them through the formalities before seeing them safely onto their connecting ﬂight.
