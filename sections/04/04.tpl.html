something for everyone

We offer meet & greet services to suit different needs, from business travellers to those with special needs.

  • bronze meet & greet

    With our bronze service, we provide a guide through airport formalities, whether arriving or departing.

  • silver meet & greet

    Recommended for young or ﬁrst-time passengers, and those with special needs, our silver service provides an escort through all the formalities and clearances. We also offer complementary porter services for help with baggage.

  • gold meet & greet

    Ideal for business travellers and those who require language assistance, this special service offers a private, dedicated escort who will accompany customers through all the airport formalities. We also provide complementary porter services to assist with baggage and marhaba lounge access during the next departure from Dubai or Bahrain.

  • family meet & greet

    This service is designed speciﬁcally for family groups and mothers travelling with young children or infants. We also provide baby strollers in all our marhaba locations* to make it easier to navigate your way through the airport.

This service is not available in Dubai International airport Terminal 2
